My career is an unconventional thread: engineering training that sharpened analytical thinking, journalism that demanded storytelling at pace and precision, and now an MBA in global marketing that connects all the dots. I've lived in newsrooms that serve 25 million viewers and classrooms across three continents. Both taught me the same thing — the most powerful ideas are the ones that get built.

I learned that great marketing and great journalism share a DNA: truth-finding, audience empathy, and compression of complexity into clarity.

Today, I'm building on that foundation — studying cross-cultural consumer behavior, experimenting with AI-driven brand narratives, and designing strategies that don't just stay on slides. They get built.

Blue Ocean Strategy · Shopify

Taillure

The Problem

The fashion e-commerce space is overcrowded with brands competing on price, trends, and visibility — creating noise but not differentiation.

The Insight

Consumers don’t just want products — they want identity, personalization, and a sense of ownership. The gap wasn’t in fashion, but in experience.

Execution

Built Taillure as a Blue Ocean brand — designing a Shopify-based platform that integrates personalization, curated styling journeys, and narrative-led product positioning. Focused on UX, brand voice, and customer journey mapping to create a differentiated digital experience.

02
Documentary · Journalism

Period Destitution

The Problem

In Bahraich, UP, over 5 lakh adolescent girls had dropped out of school due to lack of menstrual hygiene infrastructure. A crisis invisible to mainstream media.

The Insight

The story wasn't just about health — it was about how silence and stigma enforce inequality. The right frame changes everything.

Execution

Self-produced, scripted, directed and edited a full documentary. Field reported across multiple villages, conducting interviews with women, girls, and community leaders.

Result
Full documentary submitted as PG thesis. Selected as featured LinkedIn content. Showcased at Asian College of Journalism.

03
Climate Content · Journalism

Microplastics in Chennai's Seafood

The Problem

Climate science rarely breaks through to mass audiences — it's often too dense, too abstract, too removed from daily life.

The Insight

Tell the story through the fish on people's plates. Make the invisible threat tactile and personal.

Execution

Scripted, produced and directed an investigative video series on microplastic contamination found in the 7 most popular fishes sold in Chennai.

Result
Climate content series contributed to 45% engagement lift at WION. ESG report featured in journalism thesis.

Cross-Cultural Research · SP Jain

AI Without Borders

The Problem

Amazon deploys AI-driven customer experience globally — but does "global" actually mean the same thing in India, UAE, and the US?

The Insight

Cultural context shapes how AI-powered features are perceived, trusted, and used. Identical tech ≠ identical experience.

Execution

Cross-cultural analysis of Amazon's AI ecosystem across three markets — evaluating UX localisation, algorithmic trust, and consumer behavior patterns by region.

Result
MBA project for SP Jain Global Learning Committee. Developed a framework for cross-cultural AI marketing strategy.
